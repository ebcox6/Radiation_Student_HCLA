Exploring Radiation Therapy Project - Student Overview

Welcome, innovators! This project will take you through an exciting journey of exploring the principles and applications of radiation in cancer treatment and shielding. Over four weeks, students will engage in research, collaborative problem-solving, and hands-on activities to understand how radiation is used in cancer therapy, its impact on human physiology, and how effective shielding can be designed.
